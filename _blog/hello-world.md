We're excited to launch the Artificial Intelligence Institute blog. Our mission is to help workers across every industry understand and apply artificial intelligence in their daily work — no technical background required.

## What to Expect

This blog will cover:

- **Practical AI skills** — actionable techniques you can use immediately
- **Workforce insights** — how AI is reshaping jobs across healthcare, finance, retail, and education
- **Tool reviews** — honest looks at the AI tools your coworkers are already using
- **Career guidance** — how to position yourself for the AI-augmented workplace

## Why We Started This

The AI Institute is a 501(c)(3) nonprofit workforce development organization. We believe that AI literacy should be accessible to everyone — not just engineers and programmers.

Our newsletter already reaches 50,000+ workers weekly. This blog is a space for longer, deeper dives into the topics that matter most to your career.
