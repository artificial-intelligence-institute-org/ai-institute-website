About Us

Our Mission

We are a nonprofit workforce development and education organization dedicated to helping workers, communities, and institutions adapt to the rapidly changing future of work through accessible, ethical, and practical AI education.

Founded in 2023, the Artificial Intelligence Institute is a nonprofit workforce development organization bridging the AI skills gap. We partner with businesses, educational institutions, and community organizations to deliver accessible, ethical, and practical AI training at scale.

Unlike traditional academic programs, our curriculum is built around real-world applicability — designed in collaboration with industry and workforce partners to ensure learners gain skills that translate directly to career advancement and organizational growth.
