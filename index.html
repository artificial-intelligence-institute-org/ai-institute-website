About Us

Our Mission

We are a non-profit organization dedicated to making world-class AI education accessible to everyone, fostering groundbreaking research, and building open-source tools that advance the field.

Founded in 2023, the Artificial Intelligence Institute bridges the gap between cutting-edge AI research and practical education. We believe that understanding AI is not just a technical skill — it's a fundamental literacy for the future.

Through our programs, research initiatives, and open-source contributions, we aim to democratize AI knowledge and create a global community of responsible AI practitioners.
